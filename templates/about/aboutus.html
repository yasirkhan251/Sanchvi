Sanchvi Studio

About Us

Sanchvi was founded in 2019 by Sanjana Jain in Bangalore, India. The brand began as a niche rental service for exclusive maternity wear, driven by Sanjana's passion for experimental silhouettes that blend style with comfort.

Sanchvi aimed to offer expecting mothers options that were elegant and unique, breaking away from traditional norms.

The brand's reach extended beyond maternity wear to include pre-wedding rentals, catering to a broader audience.

In 2022, Sanchvi introduced a line of children’s wear, expanding its commitment to high-quality, stylish clothing for every stage of life.

The expansion into kids' wear was met with enthusiasm, further solidifying Sanchvi's reputation as a versatile and beloved fashion brand.

Customer support from around the globe has been crucial in Sanchvi’s success.

The brand’s dedication to unique, high-quality designs and its adaptability to changing clientele needs have made it a cherished name in the fashion rental industry.

Sanchvi has grown from a small idea to a global phenomenon, reflecting the passion and creativity that continue to drive the brand forward.
